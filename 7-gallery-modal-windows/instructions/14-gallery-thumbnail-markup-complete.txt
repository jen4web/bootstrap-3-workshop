To each LI, added this:
 class="col-sm-3 col-xs-4 thumbnail"

You can change the grid markup as you wish. The thumbnail class gets rid of the bullets and adds the border.

You will get a float problem, in that the left column wraps into the gallery at some dimensions. To fix, add a class of "clearfix" to the UL.
  
  http://getbootstrap.com/components/#thumbnails
